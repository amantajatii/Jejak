Jejak is not alone.

Tidak ditemukan produk yang persis menggabungkan LPBBTI Indonesia, PSrE, independent claim matching, single-use authorization lintas funder, dan DePIN. Tetapi hampir setiap komponennya sudah memiliki pemain kuat—dan beberapa analog sangat dekat.

Verdict: pasar luasnya crowded; niche persisnya sparse-to-moderate. Diferensiasi Jejak ada pada integrasi dan jaringan, bukan novelty teknis.

Escrow vs Jejak

Jejak yang direkomendasikan

Funder tetap memegang dana dan keputusan. Jejak mengeluarkan receipt VERIFIED; sistem funder membaca dan mengonsumsi receipt itu sebelum pencairan.

Jejak jika berubah menjadi escrow

Dana dikunci di kontrak/rekening sampai kondisi verifikasi terpenuhi. Ini langsung berhadapan dengan Trustless Work dan banyak escrow/payment-gating platform serta menambah risiko regulasi.

Jangan sebut “badge”. Badge terdengar reusable dan generik. Jejak membutuhkan authorization receipt yang spesifik, pendek umur, non-transferable, dan hanya dapat digunakan sekali.

Non-crypto substitutes

  • Direct Privy/VIDA + funder database: simplest and most dangerous substitute.
  • Pusdafil/SLIK: existing regulatory/industry data infrastructure; stronger institutional position, although not a pre-funding consent receipt.
  • OneSpan/BlueInk: full lending e-sign, identity and controlled workflows.
  • Ocrolus: document tampering checks plus human-in-the-loop review.

Crypto and Stellar adjacency

  • Chainlink ACE: policy and compliance attestations that gate smart-contract actions.
  • ACTA + Stellar Attestation Service: credentials and attestations on Stellar.
  • Ethereum Attestation Service: already publishes a P2P loan attestation schema.
  • Eara: identity registry and mandatory compliance hooks for Stellar assets.
  • Microvault: offchain data approval triggering Soroban lending.
  • Trustless Work: direct competitor only if Jejak becomes escrow.

Failure signal: Civic Pass

Civic Pass offered reusable onchain identity/access badges, but Civic announced its sunset in 2025 and shifted focus. Lesson: a generic verification badge can be technically valid without sustaining the product strategy.

Failure signal: TradeLens

Maersk and IBM discontinued TradeLens even after building a viable platform because full ecosystem collaboration and commercial viability were not achieved. This is Jejak's biggest warning: shared registries die without network adoption.

The narrow whitespace

Jejak masih memiliki ruang jika semua kondisi berikut dipertahankan:

  1. Indonesia LPBBTI, bukan generic global lending.
  2. PSrE-signed terms diverifikasi independen dari originator.
  3. Authorization terikat borrower, originator, agreement, amount, purpose, dan expiry.
  4. Satu authorization hanya dapat dikonsumsi satu kali oleh participating funder.
  5. PII offchain; hanya hash/status/consumption evidence onchain.
  6. DePIN hanya exception investigation, bukan pengganti TTE.

Bukan smart contract. Bukan badge. Bukan AI. Moat baru muncul ketika PSrE, originator, dan beberapa funder memakai canonical claim rules yang sama serta menghasilkan shared fraud signals.

Recommended positioning

“Jejak is Indonesia's independent funding-authorization registry: it verifies that PSrE-signed borrower consent matches the exact claim a funder is buying, and prevents that authorization from being consumed twice.”

Hindari positioning “escrow”, “KYC badge”, “government-backed”, atau “blockchain prevents all double financing”. Semuanya terlalu luas atau belum benar.
