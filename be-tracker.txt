IMPORTANT BOUNDARIES
--------------------
- Use integer Money; never floating point for financial values.
- Keep PII and legal documents off-chain and out of logs/events.
- Mutations require idempotency, optimistic concurrency, and audit records.
- Use a transactional outbox; never best-effort event publication.
- Sandbox partners must be visibly labeled in APIs, UI metadata, logs, and docs.
- BE coordinates integrations but does not own risk-model internals or Soroban logic.
- A task moves to DONE only after its acceptance criteria and tests pass.
